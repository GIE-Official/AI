# Contributing to GIE AI

Thank you for your interest in contributing to GIE AI.

## Research Contributions
We welcome contributions that improve the physical accuracy or robustness
of the Soliton‑based admissibility model.

## Code Contributions
Before submitting a pull request:

1. Ensure your modifications do not break the physical logic of the core engine.  
2. Document any theoretical or algorithmic modifications.  
3. Verify that no latency regressions are introduced.  

By contributing, you agree that your work will be licensed under the  
MIT License.
