# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to maintain a respectful, safe, and constructive environment
for all contributors.

## Standards
Acceptable behavior includes:
- Respectful communication  
- Constructive feedback  
- Technical focus  

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment or personal attacks  
- Publishing others' private information  
- Deliberate disruption  

## Enforcement
Project maintainers will review and address violations of this Code of Conduct.
Reports may be submitted confidentially to:

Raymond.ma@me.com

All reports will be handled discreetly.
